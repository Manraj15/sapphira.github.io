How It Works?

Our treatment involves an hybrid (a mix of carbamide peroxide and hydrogen peroxide) and a blue LED light. The LED light activates the gel and acts as an inhibitor to open pores of the teeth so the peroxide can break down stain in the dentin layer of the teeth. All sessions are completed in one appointment. The nurse will apply gel on teeth and have you sit under a LED light for 20 minutes. After each session under the LED light, you will rinse with water and then repeat the process 2 more times. During time of your appointment the nurse will also go through post treatment care instructions.

We also send our clients home with a House of Smiles smile stick which is Hybrid Peroxide Whitening Pen for free. It is a similar composition to the gel we use in our studio; the Smile Stick is a hybrid whitening gel with a lower concentration of peroxide to make it safe for home use.
